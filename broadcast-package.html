YC's Favourite Streamers Hightlights

Broadcast Package

Description

I was asked to create a broadcast package for my own fictional YouTube channel. The channel will show highlights of my favourite streamers. It shows the intro, a lower-third and transition.

Challenge(s)

I will need to create a logo for the YouTube channel and gather some Twitch clips to include in the video. In addition, I need to think of a concept for the intro, lower-third and transition. It needs to look consistent across the three elements.

Solution(s)

For the logo, I thought using the bubble chat from the Twitch logo because it is related to Twitch and add my ligature inside the bubble chat. Below the logo, it will have a text labelled Highlights. It will have a yellow background behind the text because it will act as a highlighter. For the introduction, I thought of showing a quick preview of the streamers will be included in the video. In addition, it will preview a clip and freeze the screen after a few second and it will show their full name and online username. The screen will be grey out except the streamer’s whole body. The lower-third will show the Twitch streamer username and their social media. The transition will act as a highlighter. So, it will be a rectangular bar that moves from the left side to right side or vise-versa.

Steps to complete the project

To start off, I created two style frames and a storyboard for the video. The style frames are static images of what it would look like for the final version. The storyboard will tell what will happen inside the video. I created my wireframes on Adobe Illustrator.

After that, I went to find Twitch clips. I actually never downloaded Twitch clips before so I had to search online on how to download Twitch clips. If you are curious, here is the link for the guide.

After I gather the clips, I imported all my clips into After Effects and start building the video.

Problem(s) encountered

The main problem that I encountered is the timing from switching clip to next clip in the introduction. Sometimes, it won’t just start from the beginning of the clip.

How I solve it?

I just took my time and work more slowly and precisely because some compositions were overlapping with each other so I had to move the compositions and fix my keyframes.
